The issue

As a minority group, Māori needs and values are often overlooked. Māori beliefs, Values and Experiences should be taken into account with decisions made around these.

The solution

The Meihana Model was developed for a Mental health situation but can be applied across the health arena. This model can be taught as well as other resources being used in order to address the issues at hand. Examples of companies can be found below as well as learning on our pages.
